\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item Add products to your Woo\+Commerce store.
\item The plugin automatically adds an {\bfseries{"{}\+Add to Enquiry"{}}} button to product pages.
\item Users can view their selected items in the {\bfseries{Enquiry Cart}}.
\item A form allows users to submit their inquiries. Both admin and the user receive email notifications.
\item Admins can view, manage, and update enquiry statuses or delete enquiries in bulk from the {\bfseries{Admin Enquiry Management}} section.
